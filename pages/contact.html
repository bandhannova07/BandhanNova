Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with BandhanNova?

Simply visit our homepage and explore our learning hubs. All courses are free and accessible immediately. Create an account to track your progress and earn certificates.

Are the courses really free?

Yes! All our courses, certificates, and learning materials are completely free. Our mission is to make quality education accessible to everyone.

Can I download the course materials?

Course content is available online for the best interactive experience. However, you can generate and download certificates upon course completion.

Do you offer support for learners?

Absolutely! We provide comprehensive support through our contact form, email, and community forums. Our team is here to help you succeed.

How can I contribute to BandhanNova?

We welcome contributions! You can provide feedback, suggest course topics, report issues, or even collaborate on content creation. Contact us for partnership opportunities.

Is BandhanNova mobile-friendly?

Yes! Our platform is fully responsive and optimized for all devices - desktop, tablet, and mobile. Learn anywhere, anytime.
